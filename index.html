About
Runna

Runna Rulez is a Zimbabwean new-wave hip-hop artist known for energetic delivery, strong visual identity and a streetwear-influenced image. His work connects music, performance, fashion and youth culture into a brand built for fans, stages and collaborations.

Sound

A fusion of trap, hip-hop and Zimbabwean street energy, driven by catchy hooks, hard drums and confident vocal delivery.

Brand

A bold identity shaped by red, black and white palettes, streetwear styling, stage imagery and high-contrast visuals.
